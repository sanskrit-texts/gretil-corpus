muṭivuṟāta pāṭalkaḷ

taṉatu tāyattiṟkāṉa inta ēkkattait tavira vēṟenta ēkkattaiyum nām nirākarikka vēṇṭumallavā? inta ēkkam eṉṉuṭaṉ irukkiṟatu. eṉakkuḷ irukkiṟatu. atu eṉatu pātattōlai uracum kaṭaliṉ veṇmaṇal pōṉṟatu. atu eṉ kaṇkaḷil, eṉ irattattil vāḻkiṟatu. eṉatu vāḻviṉ ovvoru nampikkaiyiṉ piṉṉaṇikkum ataṟkuriya parimāṇattai vaḻaṅkukiṟatu. orunāḷ, eḻutuvatai niṟuttiviṭṭu jaṉṉal
vaḻiyāka nāṉ pārppēṉ - oru raṣya ilaiyutir kālattai. - viḷāṭimir napakōv (niṉaivē, pēcu)

"tuppākki vāyppaṭṭa" taṉatu "ciṟu tēca"ttai viṭṭuk kaviñaṉ pulampeyarkiṟāṉ. tāyakattil "kāṟṟum neruppumāy, vāḻvum iruppum". "ūṉattaḻumpuka"ḷēṟiya kaviñaṉiṉ uṭalum itayamum kālattiṉ "cuḻal" pātaiyai viṭṭakaṉṟu vilakiyiruppataiyē virumpukiṉṟaṉa -tēciya viṭutalaip pōrāṭṭam kuṟitavaṟi, ilakku maṟantu, urumāṟiya kāraṇattāl. kaṭal kaṭantu māṟṟāṉ tēcattai aṇṭi vāḻntālum,

"viḻinīrp perukkuṭaṉum
āḻkaṭal pōr muṭittu
aṇikulaintu tirumpum
kaṭaṟpuravikaḷ"

koṇṭa 'nēcakkaṭaliṉ' niṉaivu kaviñaṉiṉ maṉattai viṭṭoḻiya maṟukkiṟatu. "turuvam tappi vanta" avaṉ, ippōtu "veṇtōl miṉuṅkum" mēṟkattiya corkkattil "ūril tīṇṭāta 'iḻicaṉap' paṇikaḷai" mēṟkoṇṭu kālam kaḻikkum mukamaṟṟa akati; caraṇālayam nīṅkiya ētilippaṟavai. tāyakattil avaṉatu maṉaiviyum makaṉum; viḻittuṟaṅkum tolaivukaḷil avarkaḷ iruppiṉum, avaḷ kaṇṭa cūriyaṉai avaṉ
kāṇa nēram piṭikkum. piḷḷai cirittu nittiraiyil mukam curukkumpōtu,
"nari vantu viraṭṭuteṉṟu" colla avaṉāl muṭiyātu. "kuṇṭum kuḻiyumāṉa" avaṉ piṟanta nāṭṭil maṉaivi" ūṉuruki uṭaluruki" piḷḷaiyait "taṉṉukkuḷ pōrttu" avaṉukkākak kāttiruppāḷ. kaviñaṉukku iṉi vāyttatutāṉ eṉṉa? iṉi avaṉāl vīṭu tirumpa muṭiyumā?

ki.pi. aravintaṉiṉ kavitaikaḷ mūṉṟu vakai aṉupavaṅkaḷai uḷḷaṭakkiyavai: yāḻppāṇattu aṉupavaṅkaḷ; tamiḻakattil vāḻnta iṭaikkālattu aṉupavaṅkaḷ; akativāḻviṉ pātippukaḷ (mēṟku airōppiya aṉupavaṅkaḷ). ikkavitaikaḷ varalāṟṟaṉupavaṅkaḷil tōyntu eḻuntavai maṭṭumalla. avvaṉupavaṅkaḷ alātiyāṉa oru kaṟpaṉait tiṟattāl, paḷiṅku pōṉṟatoru teḷivāṉa moḻiyāl cīramaikkappaṭṭu,
paṇpaṭuttappaṭṭuk kavitaikaḷāka vārtteṭukkappaṭṭuḷḷaṉa. pōrāḷiyiṉ cōrvu, akatiyiṉ maṉattāṅkalkaḷ
cintikkum maṉitar evarukkumē uṇṭākum aiyappāṭukaḷ, tarmacaṅkaṭaṅkaḷ- ivai oṉṟōṭoṉṟu piṉṉip piṇainta nilaiyai ikkavitaikaḷil kāṇalām. kavitai moḻi veḷippaṭumpōtu cari ('naṇpaṉukku') ilakkiyac cāṟṟil ūṟit tiḷaittu ilakkiya uṇarvukaḷai yatārttattiṉ etirē niṟutti naiyāṇṭi ceyyumpōtum cari ('pō...aṅkiru') nayamāṉa coṟkaḷaik koṇṭu oru kaṉavulakaip paṭaittuk kāṭṭumpōtu cari-

vāḻat tuṭitta oru māṉuṭak kūṭṭattiṉ piratinitikaḷāy nāṅkaḷ uṅkaḷukku aṟimukamāṉōm. '77ām āṇṭu ākasṭu kalavaram tamiḻakat tēciya caktikaḷai, emmītu aṉutāpam koḷḷac ceytatu. intiya upakaṇṭap piracciṉaikaḷil īḻap piraccaṉaiyum piṉṉip piṇaintiruppataṉāl, intiyāviṉ mukkiyattuvattai uṇarntu ematu pōrāṭṭattiṟku piṉ taḷamāy intiyāvaik koḷḷa nām eṇṇam koṇṭōm. intiyāviṉ uḷḷaka
muraṇpāṭukaḷ emmaip pātikkā vaṇṇam avarkaḷiṉ utaviyai, ātaravai vakaiyākap payaṉpaṭuttat
tiṭṭamiṭṭōm. '77iṉ ilaṅkaik kalavaram emmai ittīrmāṉattiṟku untit taḷḷiyatu. avvēḷaiyil toṭakkak kāla īḻap pōrāḷikaḷ, tēciya caktikaḷuṭaṉ tammai aṭaiyāḷappaṭuttiyiruntaṉar. paravalāṉa apippirāyattai avvēḷaiyil peṟṟirukkavillai.

'77iṉ piṉṉāl īḻat tēciya iṉap pōrāṭṭattiṉ tīrmāṉa caktiyāy tamiḻīḻa viṭutalaip pulikaḷ tammai aṭaiyāḷappaṭutti, uruppeṟat toṭaṅkiyiruntaṉar. ivvēḷaiyilēyē nāṅkaḷ iṅku aṟivujīvikaḷ mattiyil emmai aṟimukappaṭuttik koṇṭōm. īḻap pōrāṭṭattiṉ tattuva vēṟupāṭṭai, uḷ muraṇpāṭukaḷai, cikkalkaḷai, cariyāṉa ticaiyai nām puriyavaittōm. tamiḻaka aṟivu jīvikaḷil cilar emmaip purintu
koṇṭaṉar. 'tamiḻaka īḻa naṭpuṟavuk kaḻakamāy' 1977 il tammai amaippu vaṭivattiṟku
uṭpaṭuttik koṇṭaṉar. ikkaḻakamē īḻap pōrāṭṭattiṉ ātaravāḷarkaḷai amaippu vaṭivamākak koṇṭa mutal ātaravāḷar amaippākum. ivvamaippu mātāntak kūṭṭaṅkaḷai naṭattiyatu. emmuṭaṉ araciyal uṟavuṭaṉ allātu, ematu ilakkiyam, kalai, camūka iyal, varalāṟu ivaṟṟuṭaṉum uṟavu koṇṭatu. ciṟu veḷiyīṭukaḷai veḷik koṇarntatu. 'ilaṅkaiyil īḻam' eṉṉum ciṟu nūl avvēḷaiyil īḻap pōrāṭṭattai, ataṉ
varalāṟṟai putiya kōṇattil aṟimukappaṭuttiyatu. tamiḻīḻat tēciya caktikaḷ tammai tamiḻakat
tēciya caktikaḷuṭaṉ, avaṟṟaip piratipalitta kaṭcikaḷuṭaṉ tammai nerukkamāy aṭaiyāḷappaṭuttik koṇṭaṉa.

ovvoru tamiḻakak kaṭcikaḷum ovvoru tamiḻīḻa iyakkaṅkaḷai ñāṉak kuḻantaikaḷāka cuvīkarittuk koṇṭaṉa. nām ivaṟṟil iruntu otuṅki, pārvaiyāḷarkaḷāy iruntatuṭaṉ tamiḻakattiṉ muṟpōkkāḷarkaḷiṭam emmai aṭaiyāḷap paṭuttiṉōm. avarkaḷiṭam naṇparkaḷāy uṟavu koṇṭōm; tōḻamai vaḷarttuk koṇṭōm; karuttukkaḷaip paṟimāṟik koṇṭōm; vivātittōm. tāyakat tamiḻaṉ, cēyakat tamiḻaṉ eṉṟa
maṉappāṅkuṭaṉ nām uṟavu koḷḷavillai. nām iruvarum maṉita nēyattaiyē uyarttip piṭittōm.
maṉita kulattai tuṉpaṅkaḷup, tuyaraṅkaḷum cūḻukaiyil tuṉpappaṭṭōm; etirkoḷḷum pakkuvam peṟṟōm; āṟṟalai vaḷarttōm. ulakeṅkum āṉa aṭakkumuṟaikaḷai etir koḷḷum ovvoru pōrāḷiyaiyum, tōḻaṉaiyum niṉaivu koṇṭōm. avarkaḷ uṇarvukaḷuṭaṉ iṟukkamāṉōm. ap pōrāḷikaḷiṉ veṟṟikaḷ emakku uṟcākamūṭṭiṉa. avarkaḷuṭaiya aṉupavaṅkaḷai nām uḷvāṅkik koṇṭōm.
eṅkaḷukkum intiyāviṟkumāṉa, kuṟippākat tamiḻakattiṟkumāṉa uṟavu ippaṭittāṉ iruntatu. aṟivu jīvikaḷuṭaṉ ārampitta ematu uṟavu, ilakkiya amaippukaḷ, periyāriṉ karuttukkaḷai ēṟṟuk koṇṭavarkaḷ eṉa virivaṭaiyat toṭaṅkiyatu. appōtu nām 'laṅkā rāṇi' eṉṟa nāvalai veḷiyiṭṭōm.
innūl emakkup paravalāṉa toṭarpukaḷaip peṟṟut tantatu. ematu veḷiyīṭukaḷai aṉuppik koṇṭiruntōm. karuttukkaḷil iṇaintōm. āciriyarkaḷ, pērāciriyarkaḷ, eḻuttāḷarkaḷ, kaviñarkaḷ, kalai ilakkiyavātikaḷ, ṭākṭarkaḷ, iḷam vakkīlkaḷ, muṟpōkku eṇṇam koṇṭa iḷaiñarkaḷ, pallavaṉ pēruntu ūḻiyarkaḷ, pēccāḷarkaḷ, māṇavar amaippiṉar, iḷaiñar aṇiyiṉar, ciṟṟūḻiyarkaḷ, ālait toḻilāḷikaḷ, ippaṭip
pala maṭṭaṅkaḷil toṭarpukaḷ iṟukkamākiṉa. ivaṟṟai viṭavum kirāmap puṟattu
vivacāyikaḷ, iḷaiñarkaḷ karaiyōra mīṉ piṭit toḻilāḷarkaḷum emmītu aṉpu koṇṭiruntaṉar. ivarkaḷuṭaṉ kaḻinta ematu poḻutukaḷ iṉimaiyāṉavai.

ceṉṉai nakaril, kūvam natikkaraiyum, pakkiṅkām kālvāyk karaiyum nām vāḻnta pakutikaḷāy iruntaṉa. kuṭicai māṟṟu vāriyak kuṭiyiruppukkaḷē ematu vatiyiṭaṅkaḷāy iruntaṉa. inta makkaḷiṭamtāṉ nām tamiḻakattiṉ ātmāvai, maṉitatattuvattai taricittōm. nāṅkaḷ aḻukaiyil avarkaḷum aḻutārkaḷ; nāṅkaḷ cirikkaiyil avarkaḷum cirittārkaḷ; emmuṭaṉ avarkaḷ paṭṭiṉi kiṭantaṉar. ematu
perumpālāṉa cāppāṭṭup poḻutukaḷ ivarkaḷatu illaṅkaḷilēyē nikaḻntaṉa.
oruvar mutukiṉ mēl oruvar ēṟi niṉṟu cuvaroṭṭi ōṭṭiṉōm. eṅkaḷukku avaciyamāṉa nēraṅkaḷil ivarkaḷ kaṭaṉpaṭṭaṉar. nām muṉṉēṟic celvatil ivarkaḷ ārvam kāṭṭiṉar; uṟcākam tantaṉar. ciṟanta naṇparkaḷai nām iṅku peṟṟirukkiṟōm. māṟiya cūḻnilaiyil iṉṟu "īḻa naṇpar kaḻakam" mūlam nām paranta uṟavaip peṟṟirukkiṟōm. koṭiya paci vēḷaikaḷil, veḷiyil koṭumaiyil alaintu tirikaiyil,
ettaṉaik kuṭumpaṅkaḷ eṅkaḷai aravaṇaittaṉa! mukamalarcciyuṭaṉ, viruntōmpum uṇarvuṭaṉ
eṅkaḷ kavalaikaḷai, cōkaṅkaḷai, kōpaṅkaḷai varuṭik koṭuttaṉar; nāṅkaḷ avarkaḷait tāyāy, tantaiyāy, tōḻarāy, uṟaviṉarkaḷāy matittōm.

iṉṉum pularāta eṅkaḷiṉ intap poḻutilum, ettaṉaiyō niṉaivukaḷ kumiḻiṭukiṉṟaṉa. tamiḻakattiṉ māvaṭṭam tōṟum naṭaipeṟum mānāṭukaḷiṉ pantalkaḷiṉ kīḻ nāṅkaḷ uṅkaḷaic cantittōm. cantippukaḷ aṉaittilum vivātaṅkaḷ, karuttu parimāṟalkaḷ, cantēka viḷakkaṅkaḷ, caṇṭaikaḷ uṟavukaḷ! tamiḻakattiṉ iyaṟkaiyai racittōm, intiyāviṉ ciṟappukaḷai viyantōm. intiyāvil oru māṉuṭam, uḻaikkum māṉuṭam
vīḻntu kiṭakkiṟatu. ataṉ āṉmāvai emmāl taricikka muṭintatu. intiyāvil uḷḷaka
muraṇpāṭṭiṉ viḷaivukaḷāy, cōkamum, vētaṉaiyum, vēlaiyillāmaiyum, paṭṭiṉik cāvum, teruvōra vāḻvum; anta vāḻvilum avarkaḷiṉ kaḷaṅkamaṟṟa cirippum makiḻvum; avarkaḷiṉ caṇṭaikaḷaiyum, kōpaṅkaḷaiyum ataṉuḷ irukkum āṟṟalkaḷaiyum ataṉ maṉa ōcaiyaip purintu koḷḷa muṭiyāta vittakam ceyyum tattuvavātikaḷaiyum, camūka amaippiṉ kōraṅkaḷaiyum, nāṅkaḷ iṅkē kaṇṭōm.

tēnīrk kaṭai ōrattilum, cikareṭ piṭittapaṭi pattirikai puraṭṭum peṭṭik kaṭaiyilum, kai ēnti uṇavaic cuvaitta paṭiyum; kaṉṉimārāvilum, tēvanēyap pāvāṇar nūlakattilum, ataṉ makānāṭṭuk kūṭaṅkaḷilum, karuttaraṅkukaḷilum, ūrvalaṅkaḷilum nāṅkaḷ kiḷarcciyuṭaṉ cantittu koṇṭōm. kuṟṟālac cāralil naṉaintum; tēkkaṭiyilum, periyār aṇaikkaṭṭilum, mēṟku malait toṭarccik kāṭukaḷil
alaintum; ūṭṭik kuḷiril viṟaittum; koṭaikkāṉal kuḷiril paṭaku viṭṭum; kōvilpaṭṭi,
civakāci, aruppukkōṭṭai karical nilattil veyilil vāṭiyum; cēlattil, āttūril cemmaṇ parappaik kaṇṭu viyantum; vaṇṭip periyāril, kaṭalūril malaiyakattiṉ cōkattai eṇṇiyum; tīppeṭṭi uṟpattit tiṟaṉaik kaṇṭu āccariyam koṇṭum; ciṟuvarkaḷ uḻaippiṉaic curaṇṭum kōrattaikkaṇṭu tuṇukkuṟṟum; kirāmattu akalavāyk kiṇaṟukaḷilum, maṭaikaḷilum nīnti nīrāṭiyum; kampaṅkāṭṭilum, cōḷak
kollaikaḷil vayal varappukkaḷil naṭantu tirintum; kirāmaṅkaḷ
tōṟum paṇṭaiya kalaikaḷaik kāṇpataṟkāy iravukaḷ viḻittum; kummiyum, kuravaiyum kōlāṭṭamum, terukkūttum kaṇṭu racittum....

naṇparkaḷē! uṅkaḷōṭirunta niṉaivukaḷ emmaik kiḷarnteḻac ceykiṉṟaṉa.

enta niṉaivukaḷai nām innērattil uṅkaḷuṭaṉ pakirntu koḷḷa muṭiyum!

īḻam paṟṟi- īḻap pōrāṭṭam paṟṟi tamiḻaka makkaḷ kaṉavukkuḷ, māyaikkuḷ āḻttappaṭṭuḷḷaṉar. ceytikaḷ muḻumaiyāka avarkaḷukkut terivikkappaṭavillai. ārampattil eṅkaḷaic cantittavarkaḷ, 'nīṅkaḷ malaiyāḷamā pēcukiṟīrkaḷ?' eṉṟaṉar. nām tamiḻ pēcum makkaḷ eṉpataiyum, tamiḻ pēcukiṟōm eṉpataiyum nampa vaippatu eṉpatu periya ceyal eṅkaḷukku. appuṟam 'piḻaikkap pōṉavaṉ, aṅku ottu
vāḻvatu tāṉē? eṉṉa taṉi nāṭu kēṭpatu? koḻumpuc ciṅkaḷavar, yāḻppāṇat tamiḻarkaḷ
ivarkaḷukku caṇṭai, ayyō pāvam tamiḻarkaḷ; tamiḻarkaḷ kollappaṭukiṉṟaṉarē, kaṟpaḻikkappaṭukiṉṟaṉarē' eṉṟa ōlam. appuṟam, 'nāṉku kōṭit tamiḻarkaḷ nāṅkaḷ, puṟappaṭṭu vantōmāṉāl ilaṅkai tāṅkātu' eṉṟa vīrac coṟkaḷ; appuṟam, ulakukku aṟivittu viṭṭu payaṇam ceṉṟaṉar; karuppu pāṭj aṇintu tukkam koṇṭāṭiṉar; irāṇuvam taṟpōtu aṉuppappaṭṭu viṭṭatu.

pōrāṭṭam muṭintu viṭṭatu (?)

tamiḻaka makkaḷ perumūccu viṭṭaṉar; taṅkaḷiṉ vaḻamaikkut tirumpiviṭṭaṉar.

malaiyakattil 10 ilaṭcam tamiḻ pēcum makkaḷ kaiviṭappaṭṭuḷḷaṉar. avarkaḷiṉ tuyarattai ēṉ tamiḻaka makkaḷ uṇaravēyillai. ilaṅkai nāṭṭai vaḷappaṭuttiyavarkaḷ iṉṟu 'nāṭaṟṟavarkaḷ'. mūṉṟu talaimuṟaikkum mēlāka cōkattaiyē cumaiyākki, kaṉavukaḷaiyē uṇavākki vāḻntu koṇṭirukkiṉṟaṉar. 150 varuṭaṅkaḷukku muṉṉāl tamiḻaka maṇṇil iruntutāṉ uṅkaḷ cakōtararkaḷ malēciyāvukkum, piji
tīvukkum, ilaṅkaikkum kūlikaḷāka aḻaittuc cellappaṭṭaṉar. 150 varuṭaṅkaḷiṉ piṉṉāl - mūṉṟām
talaimuṟaikkup piṉṉāl-avarkaḷ iṅkē tirumpi vanta pōtu, entap pāvamum aṟiyāta avarkaḷai, tamiḻaka makkaḷ ūriṉ puṟattē otukki vaittirukkum kāṭciyaiyum kaṇṇīruṭaṉ nōkkukiṟōm. vēṟṟu moḻi mānilaṅkaḷukkuk kottaṭimaikaḷāy ivarkaḷ viṟkappaṭṭa katai em neñcattil vali ēṟpaṭuttiyuḷḷatu. ik kottaṭimaikaḷai mīṭka avarkaḷiṉ tuyaraṅkaḷai araṅkattiṟkuk koṇṭu vanta tamiḻaka naṇparkaḷ eṅkaḷ
niṉaiviṟku varukiṉṟaṉar. ivarkaḷai naṇparkaḷāy peṟṟatil nām perumaiyaṭaikiṟōm.

tamiḻaka makkaḷ iṉṟu veṟum vīra vaḻipāṭṭiṟkuḷ mūḻki uḷḷaṉar. tuppākki ēntiya īḻap pōrāḷikaḷiṉ paṭaṅkaḷ tamiḻaka makkaḷ mattiyil perum varavēṟpup peṟṟiruntatu. ēṉaiya camutāyak kuḻukkaḷai viṭavum, tamiḻaka makkaḷ vīrak kaṉavukaḷil āḻvatilum, vīra vaḻipāṭṭil mūḻki viṭuvatilum, taṅkaḷ vīrattait tuṟantu viṭṭaṉar. maturai vīraṉum, karuppacāmiyum, cila kula teyvaṅkaḷum avarkaḷ
vaḻipāṭṭiṟkuriya cilai vaṭivaṅkaḷ. aṉṟaiya kulam kātta vīram ceṟintavarkaḷāka,
taṉi maṉitarkaḷai uyarttip piṭikkappaṭṭatai varalāṟṟup poruḷ mutalvātam terinta nām eppaṭi maṟantu viṭa muṭiyum? anta māyaikkuḷ irunta avarkaḷai veḷikkoṇarvatutāṉ evvaḷavu kaṭiṉam eṉpatil nām peṟṟa aṉupavaṅkaḷai maṟakka muṭiyumā? inta māyaikkuḷ cikkātavarkaḷ ātalāl tāṉē nīṅkaḷ eṅkaḷukku naṇparkaḷāṉīrkaḷ!

vaṭamarāṭcit tuṉpattai viṭavum, kiḻakku mākāṇa vivacāyikaḷ peṟṟa tuṉpaṅkaḷ koṭumaiyāṉavai. avarkaḷiṉ pōrāṭṭa vāḻvu makattāṉatu; avarkaḷ tuyaraṅkaḷ aḷappariyaṉa. mūtūrilum, maṭṭakkaḷappu kokkaṭṭic cōlaiyilum, taṅkavēlāyutapurattilum avarkaḷ maraṇattuḷ vāḻntārkaḷ. āṉāl tamiḻaka makkaḷum avarkaḷiṉ taḷapatikaḷum inta makkaḷiṉ tuyarattiṉ pōtu meḷaṉamāka iruntaṉar. ematu itayattil
rattam kacintatu. yāḻppāṇa viḷamparattiṟkuḷ, anta makkaḷ aṭainta cōkam
maṟaikkappaṭṭa pōtu, naṇparkaḷē! nīṅkaḷ tāṉ eṅkaḷ kavalaikaḷil paṅku koṇṭīrkaḷ.

pōrāḷikaḷ vāṉattil iruntu kutitta tēvarkaḷ eṉṟutāṉ tamiḻaka makkaḷ emmai nampiyiruntaṉar. vīra vaḻipāṭṭil mūḻki irukkum camutāyattil itu tavirkka muṭiyātatu ākum. tamiḻaka āṭci māṟṟaṅkaḷaik kavaṉippōr, inta āṭci māṟṟaṅkaḷ vīra vaḻipāṭṭu maṉappāṉmaiyiṉ paṅkāyiruppatai uṇaralām. ik karuttai nām uṭaikka muyaṟci ceytōm. nāṅkaḷ pōrāḷikaḷ; irattamum cataiyumāṉa maṉitarkaḷ; intac
camūka amaippiṉ viḷai poruṭkaḷ intac camūka amaippiṉ eccaṅkaḷ eṅkaḷaiyum oṭṭiyuḷḷaṉa;
pōrāḷikaḷai, pōrāṭṭattai ivvakaiyilēyē nōkkuṅkaḷ eṉṟu aṭittuc coṉṉōm. āṉāl naṇparkaḷē! uṅkaḷāl tāṉ anta uṇmaiyai uṇarntu koḷḷa muṭiyum. tamiḻaka makkaḷum, pōrāḷikaḷiṉ camūka virōtac ceyalkaḷaiyum, caṭṭa oḻuṅku mīṟalaiyum kaṇṭu mukattaic cuḻittaṉar; ēḷaṉamāy nōkkiṉar. nāṅkaḷ aṉiccamalarāy curuṅkip pōṉōm.

iccamūka amaippai, ataṉ kuṇāmcattai, ataṉ veḷippāṭukaḷaip purintu koṇṭa naṇparkaḷ eṅkaḷai aravaṇaittaṉar. ātaravu kāṭṭiṉar.

īḻattiṉ uḷḷaka muraṇpāṭukaḷ uṅkaḷukku iṅkē maṟaikkappaṭṭuḷḷaṉa; nāṅkaḷ avaṟṟai veḷippaṭaiyākavē uṅkaḷ muṉ vaittōm. tēciya iṉap piraccaṉaiyiṉ kūrmaikkuḷ uḷḷaka muraṇpāṭukaḷ maṟaikkappaṭṭiruntaṉa. ivaṟṟai kēḷvippaṭṭa vēḷaiyil uṅkaḷukkuk kacappākat tāṉ iruntatu. ēṉeṉil iṅkirunta uḷḷaka muraṇpāṭṭiṉ kōraṅkaḷ uṅkaḷai mikavum pātittiruntaṉa. tēciya iṉap pōrāṭṭattiṉ uccattil uḷḷaka
muraṇpāṭukaḷiṉ kōraṅkaḷ nīṅkiya oru camutāyam īḻattil amaiya vēṇṭumeṉa
nīṅkaḷ virumpiṉīrkaḷ. ataṟkākavē īḻap pōrāṭṭattai tirikaraṇa cuttiyuṭaṉ ātarittīrkaḷ. ēṟṟattāḻvum, cātiya vēṟupāṭum, curaṇṭalum, piṟpōkkut taṉaṅkaḷum nīṅkiya oru īḻattai uruvākka nāṅkaḷum, nīṅkaḷum kaṉavu kaṇṭōm. āṉāl intiya cutantirap pōrāṭṭa vēḷaiyil, oru kaṭṭattil pārati pāṭiya pāṭal oṉṟu ēṉō ivvēḷaiyil eṅkaḷukku niṉaiviṟku varukiṟatu:

"taṇṇīr viṭṭō vaḷarttōm carvēcā ippayirai,
kaṇṇīrāl vaḷarttōm karukattiruvuḷamō."
ivvarikaḷ eṅkaḷukku mikac cariyākap poruntumā eṉpatu
teriyavillai.

naṇparkaḷē! itu viṭaipeṟum nēramtāṉā eṉpatu tīrmāṉamākavillai. āṉālum eṅkaḷ niṉaivukaḷ uṅkaḷiṉ aṉpu, naṭpu, tōḻamai ivaṟṟaiyē tāṅkiyuḷḷaṉa. uṅkaḷiṭamum ivaṟṟaiyē kaiyaḷittuḷḷōm.
