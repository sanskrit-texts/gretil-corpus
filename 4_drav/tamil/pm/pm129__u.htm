tiruvāṭutuṟai ātīṉattu mahāvitvāṉ
tiricirapuram śrī mīṉāṭcicuntaram piḷḷaiyavarkaḷiṉ
pirapantattiraṭṭu - mutal pākam
mūlam:
tirukkailāyaparamparait tiruvāṭutuṟaiyātīṉattu mahāvitvāṉ tiricirapuram śrī mīṉāṭcicuntaram
piḷḷaiyavarkaḷ pirapantattiraṭṭu, itu mēṟpaṭi ātīṉattut talaivarkaḷākiya śrīlaśrī vaittiyaliṅka
tēcikaravarkaḷ utaviyaikkoṇṭu, mēṟpaṭi piḷḷaiyavarkaḷ māṇākkarum, citamparam
mīṉāṭci tamiḻkkālēj piriṉspālumākiya u.vē. cāminātaiyarāl ceṉṉai kamarṣiyal
accukkūṭattiṟ patikkappeṟṟatu. iraṇṭām patippu, 1926.

uḷḷaṭakkam


1. tirutturuttik kacci viṉāyakar patikam (ceyyuḷ 1- 10)
2. tirutturuttic cuppiramaṇiyasvāmi patikam (ceyyuḷ 11 - 20)
3. tiruviṭaimarutūr marutavāṇar tōttirappatikam (ceyyuḷ 21 - 30)
4. tirupperumaṇanallūrt tiruveṇṇīṟṟumai piḷḷaittamiḻ (ceyyuḷ 31 - 133)



1. tirutturuttik kacci viṉāyakar patikam


(itu cōḻanāṭṭiṟ kāvirinatiyiṉteṉpāluḷḷa tiruppatikaḷuḷ oṉṟu. kuṟṟālameṉa ikkālattuvaḻaṅkum)

1.
māmēvu pavaḷā calakkuvaṭ ṭutayameḻu maḻavilaṅ katirnikarttu
maṇimeḷali poliyalav varaimuṭiyi ṉirupālu maṇikoḻit tiḻiyaruliyiṟ
pamēvu karuñimi ṟiraippak kapōlam paruttuviṭu tāṉamoḻukap
paṉimatip piraiyoṉ ṟaṭuttamarn tāleṉap pakaroru maruppilaṅkat
tēmēvu malariṟait taṭiyarcaya cayaveṉṟu ticaitoru muḻakkeṭuppac
ceṟuvalik karumūṭi kattivarn tiyāṅkaṇmakiḻ tēkkavan taruḷ purikuvāy
kāmēvu taṇṇaḷi yamaiyintaṭaiyu narppātu kāttaṟam vaḷarttiṭutalāṟ
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

2.
kulavutava muṉivararka ḷamarun tavaccālai kuvalayam paṭitarāmē
kūṟumaccālaiyuṭ caivā kamntīṭṭu kōcaṅkaḷ citaitarāmē
nilavutaḻal paṭukuṇṭam vētimē kalaiyoṭu nitamputi tiyaṟṟuṟāmē
nittiyamu ṉārayakaru mampalavu mattavar nikaḻttaliṟ kuṟaivarāmē
pulavukama ḻaṅkucam pācaṉamai yēliyatu pōtumeṉ ṟuvakaimaliyap
poṅkuñ ciṉattulavu karumūṭi kattup porukkeṉa vivarntupalavuṅ
kalavupala vaṇṭat tulāvarun tiṟaluṭaik karuṇaiyā maṟiya valamō
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

3.
ētiyeṉa vevvaḷa viruppaṉa vavaṟṟā liruttalcey yātavaramumi
maiyava ruṭampumuta lettuṇai yuṭampavai irintiṭaṟ kāyavaramu,
nītitaru maṭṭāṅka pañcāiṅka mutaliya nikaḻttuvan taṉaivilakku
nikaḻumaṟ ṟuḷḷavu niṉaintumic ciravuruva nilavamaṟ ṟoṉṟamaintu,
cōtitaru kōṭoṉṟu koṇṭukaya mukaṉvali tolaittuliṟa lūrtiyākkiyic
cōrvilava ṉamarar pāṟ peṟṟavaiyu mēṟṟaruḷ curantupal luyirceyviṉaiyaik,
kātinalamēyutavu niṉatuperu māṇpevar karuttamait totavallār
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

4.
ēṟṟamiku morumiṉiva ṉōmpaḻaṟ kuḻināppa ṇiriyāta kiriyaiñāṉa
meṉṉumiru cattika ḷoṭuntōṉṟi yammuṉiyo ṭēṟṟamar purintupalaruñ,
cāṟṟavoḷir puravumaṇi kaikkoṇṭa māṟṟalaṉ ṟaṉvali yaṭaṅkāpperun
tāṉaivali yoṭukavarn tammaṇiyum vāṅkiyat tāpataṟ kiṉitaḷikkat,
tēṟṟamata ṉālvara luṇarntaṉaiya tavaṉuñ ciṟantamār piṟṟarittuc
cintā maṇikkuñca rappirā ṉeṉṟēttal ceyyalavaṉ viṉaiyaṉaittuṅ,
kāṟṟaveḻu maruḷpoḻin tiṉitamaru māṭalaik kaṇṭuviṇ ṭiṭavallarār
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

5.
teḷḷamuta mayamāka vumaiyammai nalkiya tiruntumō takanñcamāyt
tīyakoṭu nañcamaya mākavoru pētaitaru ceḻunāva laṅkaṉikaṭā
meḷḷalaru mamutamāyt tāṉpurita ṟavaṟā tiyaṟṟiṭak kaṇṭōmiṉu
miyalutta māṅkamvē ṟākavum pārkkavu mirucevika ḷāṟkēṭkavu
muḷḷaporu ḷituveṉṟu vāypēca vumpiṟa vuñaṟṟavu miyaṅkalceyavu
muṇmaiyiṟ kaṇṭuḷē meṉṉiṉevar niṉṉāṭa luṭkoṇ ṭaḷakka vallār
kaḷḷamakaṉ mātavark kāṉanta māypparavu kaṟpaṉai kaṭantavāḻvē
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

6.
entavā ṉavararuḷ peṟakkaruti ṉālumuṉai yēttivaḻi paṭuvareḻuvā
yēttivaḻi paṭalceyā reṉṉiṉava reṇṇiya tiḻantumiṭai yūṟuṟuvarāṉ
muntavār vamperuku māṟuni ṉaṭipppūcai muṟṟum purintupōṟṟiṉ
muṉṉiya vaṉaittumuṟ ṟātavala vēyinta muṟaimainā ṉuṇartalālē
pantamā malakaṉma māyaiyoru mūṉṟaiyum pāṟṟiniṉ ṉaṟivutoḻilām
pātaniḻal pukkiṉpa muṟumāṟu niṉṉaiyē paṟṟit tutittalceyvēṉ
kantamā malariṭuka reyppilvaip pēpukaḻnar kaḷaikaṇē yamalavāḻvē
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

7.
maruvupala puvaṉattu mentavā ṉalarvaṭivu maṇcilai vaṭuttāramoṇ
mācaika ḷivaṟṟuḷoṉ ṟāṟceytu vayiṟaḷaviṉ maruvāma laiiḵkumeṉṉiṉ ,
veruvumika vāyntnta vaṭivaruc caṉaiceyār viṭuvarav vāṟutērtal
vēṇṭātu pērutaram vāyntaniṉ ṟiruvuruva mētakap pūcaipurita,
loruvutal ceyātavarkaḷ yāvarē yeṉiṉumava rumparmaka vāṉmalariṉmē
loruvaṉmutalōrkaḷiṉu matikareṉ ṟarumaṟai yuraittalcila reṇṇāmaiyeṉ
karuvutavir mātavar kacintēt teṭukkavoḷir karuṇaiyā ṉantvāḻvvē
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

8.
poṅkuṅ karuṅkaṭal parantateṉ ṉeṉṟupala puvaṉamumo raiyameytap
pukkakaṭa ṉiṉṟumalai yiṉṟeḻun taḵteṉāp porutoḻil valāriyeṇṇit,
taṅkun tiṟaṟkoṭiya catakōṭi yollait taṭaṟṟuniṉ ṟuruvalceyyac
cakamuḻuva tumputai paṭacciṟai virikkumoru taṟukaṭkalāpamayiṉmē,
leṅkuṅ katirkkumoru pavaḷāca lampora livarntupala paṭaiyoṭumeti
rēṟṟamāṟ ṟalaṉāka vappā riṭaikkaviḻa veḻukāka mōṭukaḻukuṅ,
kaṅkum pakaḻntaṭai liṟaṟpaṭaikai toṭṭaniṉ kāmarviṟal yāvar pukaḻvār
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

9.
tutikaiyō ṭiruceviyi ṉiṉṟumoru mūvarait tōṟṟiṉā yaṇṭamaṇuvāt
tōṟṟiṭa vuyarntāyor caṉakaṉiṭu muṉavelān tuyttu miku paciterittu,
matikkaiko ṭavaṉpaṇiya mupporuḷi ṇuṇmaiyai vakuttavaṟ kuṟanaviṉṟāy
vaiyamutal yāvumumō raṟukaḷavu pōtāta vaṇṇan terittalceytāy,
vitikkaipuri vāṉunika rātapuru cuṇṭimutaṉ mēlōrkaḷ palariṟaiñca
viḻaiyumā ṉantamaya mēyāy viḷaṅkiṉāy veyyēṉiṉ ṉuṇmaitērntu,
itikkaiyuṟu māṟupē raruḷpuriva teṉṟukalaikāṇā vatītamutalē
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

10.
malaiyumoru poruḷaṉ ṟeṉakkaṟik kuntiṟal vayaṅkuṉiṉ ṉūrtivāḻka
malarporuvu kaittalap pācāṅku cappaṭai vayaṅkiyeṉ nāḷumvāḻka
nilaimaruvu ñāṉamuṅ kiriyaiyu meṉakkalai nirampiṉa reṭutturaikku
nēyamiku cittiput tikaḷeṉuñ cattika ṇirampucī rōṅkivāḻka
valaikaṭa luṭuttapuvi muḻutuniṉa taruḷaṭain tātaram perukivāḻka
vaṟputam virāvuniṉ ṉaṇiyalpū ṇākiya varuntavat tevaiyumvāḻka
kalaivaḻi niṉaipparavu ceṅkuntar marapuṅ katittunī ṭūḻivāḻka
kaṟpat talattiṉum viruttit turuttiyamar kaccik kaḷiṟṟu mukaṉē.

tirutturuttik kaccivināyakarpatikam muṟṟiṟṟu



2. tirutturuttic cuppiramaṇiyasvāmipatikam


(itu cōḻanāṭṭiṟ kāvirinatiyiṉteṉpāluḷḷa tiruppatikaḷuḷ oṉṟu. kuṟṟālameṉa ikkālattuvaḻaṅkum)

11.
pūmēvu camayaṅka ḷōrā ṟiṉukkum pulaṅkoḷuṅ kāṭcinalkap
polintaṉa veṉccolun tirumuka moraṟum polaṉceymaṇi muṭiyorāṟum
pāmēvu ceṅkati rirāṟunika rāmeṉap pakarmaṇik kuḻaiyirāṟum
paṉṉiru taṭampuyap peruvaraiyi ṉaruviyiṟ pacunara voraḻūkkutoṭaiyu
māmēvu kāṉavar kulakkōtai yoṭumēlai vāṉavar kulakkōtaiyum
vaḷaroḷiya vaṭivēlu mārpiṉu lumpoliya mayilvara kaṉattuvaruvā
temmēvu colaipuṭai kuḻun turuttit tiruttaḷiyi lamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

12.
nīṭumaka mēruvait ticaivarai yoṭuṅkoṇṭu nilavupan tāṭalceyavunēmimāl
varaiyaiyoru kaikkaṭaka mākkoṇṭu nikaramaṟ ṟoṉṟāyavu,
mōṭukati raicciṟu matāṇiyeṉa mārpiṭai yuṟappuṉaiya vuñcalatika
ḷōrēḻu moruviraṟ koṭuteṟit tilaiyeṉa voḻikkavun ticaiyyeṭṭiluṅ,
kūṭukaḷi ṟēṭṭut tutikkaikaṇ muṭintorukai koṇṭiḻut tiṭavumvalla
kulavīira vākuvait tampiyeṉa makiḻuniṉ koṟṟamevar muṟṟa vuṇarvār,
cēṭumali kāvirit teṉpāṟ ṟurutti tiruttaḷiyu ḷamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

13.
poruvariya mātavat torunāra tappeyar puṅkamuṉi purimakattup
pōntupala vaṇṭamu miṭittut takarttunuṇ puḻuticey tulāvukiṉṟa
veruvariya ceṟimayirt tirukukōṭ ṭuttakariṉ viṟalkaṇṭu naṭunaṭuṅki
mēlaivā ṉavaraṭain tōlameṉa varukupoli vīravā kuvainōkkiyap
paruvamiku takariṉai paṟṟivaru keṉṉap paṇippavava nutavavēṟip
pāriṭam pōtāmai teritarac ceṇṭāṭu paṇpeva reṭuttumoḻivār
tiruvamiku kalvicciṟappuyar turutti tiruttaḷiyu ḷamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

14.
veḷḷiyaṅ kayilaiyiṉ mutaṟcikari vāytaliṉ vinōtap poruṭṭa marunāḷ
littakaṟ cēvaicey tañcali ceyātēkum vitiyiṉaṟi yāmai yuḷḷat,
tuḷḷiyiva ṉaippaṟṟi vāveṉa vuraikkavāṅ korukuṟaṇmu ṉuyppanōkki
yoḻiiyā vakantaiyuḷai yettoḻil valāyeṉa vuñaṟṟutalil vallēṉeṉa,
veḷḷiyo rirukkurai yeṉakkuṭilai muṉcola viyampiyataṉuṭporuḷeva
ṉiyamputi yeṉatteri tarāmaiyāṟ ceṉṉiyiliraṅkap puṭaittaṭaittāy,
teḷḷiyaru meṉṉuṇarvar niṉpukaḻ turuttit tiruttaḷiyu ḷamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

15.
perukukoṭu vañcanavil kurukuvarai nilaitapap peruvāṉ kākkumāṟu
pērama ruñaṟṟumaḷa villā viṟaṟṟakuvar piṇavarai yurntutōṉṟak,
karukuniṟam vāyntukarai koṉṟiraṅ kumperuṅ kaṭaṉmuḻutum vaṟṟa vaṉaiyār
kāyattu niṉṟumveḷi vantaneyt tōreṉuṅ kaṭaṟirai yeṟintuperuka,
murukumali mālaip puyattaṉaiyar kuṭikoṇṭa mutunakara matupāḻpaṭa
muṉṉit tutikkumvā ṉavarkuṭiko ṇakarkaḷi mukantumēṉ mēṟṟaḻaitarat,
tirukutavi rayiṟoṭuniṉ viṟaleṉ ṟuruttit tiruttaḷiyu ḷamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

16.
poruntiya tamiḻkkellai yākumvaṭa vēṅkaṭap pūtaramu mātaravi ṉāṟ-
pōtumūṉ ṟumpōtu muṉṟutavu veṟpum polintuyar paraṅkuṉṟamu,
maruntiya naṟumpaḻa mutirkkum poḻiṟkiriyu mavircanta ṉācalamumel
lalar poruñ ceṅkaiyiṟ ṟaṇṭukoṭu maḻavaṭi vamaintaruḷ koḻikkum varaiyu,
maruntiyavu ḷārmāntal pōṟpirā ṉorumoḻi vayapporuṇ maṉaṅkoḷḷuvāṉ
vāymalarn taruḷiyamar mālvaraiyu maṟṟunī maruvumaruḷ yāvaruṇarvār,
tiruntiya vaḷampala virāvun turuttit tiruttaḷiyu ḷamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

17.
kaṉimaruvu vēṭṭuvar kulakkoṭi paṭarntumakiḻ kalavat taḻaintataruvē
kaṟpakap poḻilāṭu teyvatap piṭiyaik kalantviḷai yāṭukaḷiṟē
yaḷimaruvu mātava ruḷakkamala malarkāppa ṇaḷḷūṟa vūṟutēṉē
yāyiraṅ katiroruṅ kutayameḻa leṉavaṭi yaṭaintavark karaṅkāṭciyē
vaḷimaruvu puṭaviyi ṉayarntōti luṅkaruṇai maḻaipoḻi ceḻuṅkoṇṭalē
vantit tuḷāraiyev vāṉavaru meññāṉṟum vantikka vaikkunitiyē
teḷimaruvu niṉṉaṭik kaṉparu ṭuruttit tiruttaḷiyu ḷamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

18.
mūvarmuta lāyapara civaṉmēṉi yiṟpāti muṉivariya tākkavarntu
mūtaṇṭa puvaṉap parappiṟ carācara mukiḻttiṭak karuṇaiceytum,
yāvaretu karutiṉā rataiyap paṭikkavarta mitayat tamarntaḷittu
miccaiñā ṉaṅkiriyai paraiyāti yaivarmuta leṇṇilcat tikaḷākiyam ,
pūvarmuta lōrtoḻilaṉaittumoru taṉṟoḻiṟ poliveṉac ceytumiṉṉum
pukalaḷakil cattiyuḷa tuṉatukaic cattiyē pukaṉnaḷa viṭappaṭuvatō,
tēvarpala muṉivarka ṭutikkun turuttit tiruttaḷiyu ḷamaraṇṇalē
cīṟumvem pakaiyōvu māṟuvaṉ poṭukūvu cēvalaṅ koṭiyāḷaṉē.

19.
attaroru moḻiyuṇmai yiṟṟeṉat teḷiyumā ṟaruḷuniṉ kuravu pukaḻkō
vaḻakiya kuṟakkōtai pāṟporuḷi ṉiyalpaṟiya vāṟṟipani ṉāṭalpukaḻkō,
muttarkaru naccū cerintamarar ciṟaimīṭṭa muṉiviṉaṉ vākaipukaḻkō
mūkaiyeṉa vaikiyaṟi vōṅkunā valarkalaka muḻutaṟut taruḷalpukaḻkō,
pattarpala reṇṇiya paṭikkavar muṉañceṉṟu parivaṉīyaruḷalpukaḻkō
payaṉuṟā nāyiṉēṉ yātiṉai yaṟintoru payaṉpaṭap pukaḻavallēṉ,
